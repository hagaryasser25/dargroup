ABOUT DARGROUP

Dar Group for construction was established in 1997 with a group of architects, constructors and technicians to provide technical services in the field of construction, architecture and design of high quality projects. Over the years Dar Group has specialized in the high quality turn-key projects including all trades of electromechanical for buildings, housing and administrative projects , such a scompanies head quarters,exhibitions,villas,resorts,lounges and retail stores.

Philosophy

In a partnership, each person has his own skills, interests and specialties. This combination allows Dar Group to offer fully projects that express their client’s desires while adding a spirit and a style of their own, always keeping in mind the essential ideas of comfort, modernity and practicality. Attention to details in layouts, spaces, materials, custom designed furniture, accessories and fabrics, Dar applies the intangible rules of sophistication to their work by using the subtle alliance of contemporary and classical references while carefully listening to their clients’ wishes in order to create sophisticated and livable interiors. what matters most to Dar, is conveying the harmony that will exist between the owners and their home once the work is completed. “Home should be a place to live, not to be a cold space or an art gallery. What it should be is, a well designed, inviting and discreetly luxurious space where one feels comfortable living and entertaining”.

Vision

For over more than 15 years, Dar Group has been driven by the pursue of quality and intelligence. We use the creativity, imagination and knowledge of our entire team to the design and construction of each building with the highest quality. We believe that the quality of our surroundings directly influence the quality of our lives. We seek a long term relationship with our selected clients. This demonstrates the way we think, the way we are organized and the way we work, as only through working as a team, we can achieve the high standards which our culture demands. Picture a delivery process that maximizes value, early competion with no surprises and no conflict. We shape a better world as we hope to enhance prosperity, quality of life and deliver value.

The Team

In 1997 Dar Group started with a staff of an average 70 of highly qualified architects, constructors, and technicians; in addition to a group of subcontractors who developed significantly over the years to become a highly skilled professional team, only to provide unique and professional quality in the construction field. Most of Dar Group employees are certified in green buildings design as (LEED) accredited professionals by the council. The staff is trained and experienced with respect to commissioning and (LEED) requirements.
