FURNITURE , FIXTURE AND EQUIPMENT

After we finish designing your floor plans, we begin developing a color scheme, a mood board that communicates your brand message, your taste, our vision, sourcing furniture, fixtures, accessories and materials. Custom furniture may be designed where needed. Materials for flooring, walls, counters and cabinetry are selected. A lighting concept is developed. Drawings, samples and conceptual images are gathered. Quotes and pricing are researched. Your needs, budget, and brand are all considered in every product and design element we present to you.
We collaborate with our network of artisans, artists and craftsmen; people who are dedicated to innovation and quality. We commission sought after limited editions, but we are also experienced at working to a commercial structure with the best and most competitive workshops globally, allowing our designs to be produced in volume. Our procurement services provide best value for our customers. we strive to perfectly light our projects and understand the properties of light and the way it enhances the interiors of our projects.

1.F,F&E Alternative specifications & cost estimation

Once our design concept for your space is ready to present, we will schedule a meeting. We will go through the design and you will be presented with materials, drawings, photos and samples for each element in the design. we will produce preliminary options for new furniture and furnishings’ selections according to design and needs with proprietary & descriptive specifications. We will also obtain samples of fabrics and finishes for the selected items if possible, and after the approval, we shall prepare the cost estimation for the final approval.

2.Scheduling

A time-table is developed for purchasing and ordering work to be done by trades, and final installation. This schedule will be adjusted along the way to accommodate unforeseen circumstances.

3.Procurement

we shall review dealer’s offer which indicates products’ types, quantities, and prices for all the individual products specified according to the approved interior design. Also we shall review submittals to check finishes’ and fabrics’ selections. And if necessary at times, we will produce fast recommendation for substitutions or changes for items with a long lead time to meet the move in schedule.

4.Installation & Accessorizing

This is where the vision becomes reality. All work by the trades has been completed.Now the furnishings are delivered, merchandise is placed and art is hung. Accessorizing is what elevates your space to magazine-quality status. It involves adding finishing touches that complete your brand message and make your space feel complete. This phase follows a different process than the rest of the design. We will purchase the remaining items needed to take your space over the top and prepare them for professional photography.
